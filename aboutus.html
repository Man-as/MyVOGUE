Why to choose us?

MyVOGUE is the most valuable brand in our country today.The company has worked hard on our strategy of success, with measures such as instant fashion,savings in advertising,adjusted prices and brand image,among others.The main reason for the success of our company is the instant fashion. This system consists of renewing the collections every week or every two weeks, instead of every 3 months as most designers do. All this is possible thanks to the work that is done on the street, with trend collectors who see what people want.Then this is combined with its high logistics capacity, with factories and warehouses in all parts of the world. This allows us to have clothes quickly in all our stores, with very short production times. This works because we always have clothes that are worn and nothing goes out of style.Unlike other brands that have managed to sacrifice quality, we have managed to offer clothing that works almost like luxury at reasonable prices. Instead of outsourcing all the production in cheap labor countries, we have factories all over the world, outsourcing the basics and manufacturing many quality clothes in our country with the best professionals.Because we save money on advertising and warehousing, have automated processes and use economies of scale, we manage to adjust prices a lot. We manage to sell a garment that has the perfect balance between the highest quality and a reasonable price.In the end, for a brand, reputation is everything and this company has it. The brand has been operating for many years and has been associated with quality, something that makes everyone end up buying from it. The secret? Luxury within everyone’s reach, in addition to partnering with major brands in the world by acquiring buildings in the best places.
